Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Short version: We don't collect personal data ourselves. The App uses Google AdMob to show ads, which may collect certain device and ad interaction data as described below.

1 Information We Collect

We (BullBear Games) do not directly collect any personal information.

The App does not:

  • Require account registration or login
  • Collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifier
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • Send any game data to external servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies of our own

2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using Android SharedPreferences:

Data Purpose
Game balance and statisticsTrack your in-game progress
Settings preferencesRemember your chosen game settings
Achievements unlockedTrack game milestones
Leaderboard scoresDisplay your personal best scores
Player profile (name, persona)Personalise the game experience

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the App or using the "Reset" option in Settings

3 Virtual Currency

BullBear Battle uses a virtual currency (₹ Rupees) that has no real-world monetary value and cannot be exchanged for real money or goods. No real financial transactions take place.

5 Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The Google AdMob SDK may collect device identifiers for ad serving purposes as described in Section 6. The content rating is Everyone.

6 Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements between trades. AdMob is operated by Google LLC and may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising Identifier)
  • Device information (device model, OS version, screen size)
  • IP address (used to derive approximate location at city/region level)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Ad frequency: The first ad appears after 10 completed trades, then every 5 trades thereafter. Ads never interrupt an active trade.

Opting out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization" (or "Delete advertising ID" on Android 12+).

For more information about Google AdMob's data practices, please see:

  • Google AdMob & AdSense policy
  • How Google uses data from apps

6.1 Specific to iOS Users

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, the App will request your permission to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies. If you decline, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads, and your Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) will not be accessed.

7 Security

No personal data is collected or transmitted by the App itself. All local game data is stored in Android's standard app storage, protected by the operating system's security model. Network communication used by Google AdMob for ad serving is encrypted via HTTPS.

7.1 Third-Party Data Deletion Requests

Users may request the deletion of any data processed by third-party services (AdMob) by contacting us at bullbeargames@gmail.com. We will facilitate the request with the service provider in accordance with their policies.
